Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto Pasta is a pasta based dish made with pesto and pasta as the primary ingredients, pesto pasta is super quick to make and very filling as it is a pasta based dish. It is a great quick lunch or dinner meal and is delicious when served with parmesan cheese and chilli flakes!

Ingredients

  • Pasta (preferably penne)
  • Pesto, any pesto is fine but sundried pesto is the best!
  • Olive Oil
  • Dried Chili Flakes
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and add salt and olive oil to water, allow water to come to a rolling boil.
  2. Add pasta to pot once water is boiling and leave pasta for 12 minutes or until fully cooked.
  3. Drain water and add pasta back into empty pot.
  4. Add pesto to pot and grate parmesan into pot, mix all ingredients together.
  5. Add chilli flakes to pasta and mix thouroughly.
  6. Serve with grated parmensan on top.
